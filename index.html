What is Yum Yum Whey?

Yum Yum Whey is a whey protein concentrate with a low sugar content that boasts a delicious taste and a unique ingredient profile. It is therefore guaranteed to please every woman who follows a healthy diet. The main advantage of this protein powder is its high content of quality proteins, which contribute to the growth and maintenance of muscle mass. Moreover, they help maintain normal bone condition. This is sure to be appreciated by every woman who would like to take care of a functional musculoskeletal system and shapely figure, so that she can show off her six-pack or a round butt.

In the same way, this protein powder is also suitable to be a part of your diet during weight loss. This is because it is enriched with selected fat burners. It also contains the popular CLA and L-carnitine tartrate, which participates in the metabolism of fatty acids (fats) and energy production. Another advantage can be the high content of fibre in this protein powder.

Whey protein powders are generally considered to be well digestible. Despite this, we have decided to enrich our Yum Yum Whey with the enzyme bromelain. It ranks among proteolytic enzymes that help break down proteins into better digestible amino acids. However, the included MCT oil has also a role to play. The latter belongs to a group of exceptional fats with medium chain length. Unlike conventional oils, it is quickly digested, and your body can immediately use it as a source of energy for any kind of activity.

That said, we also can't forget about the important function that the minerals and vitamins contained in this protein powder have. For example, vitamin C contributes to the maintenance of optimal immune functions during and after physical activity. It is equally important for collagen production, the functioning of bones, skin and the nervous system, as well as mental well-being.

Additionally, it helps protect cells from oxidative stress, reduce fatigue and exhaustion, and increases the absorption of iron. This helps support the proper function of immunity and optimal production of both red blood cells and hemoglobin in the body. Optimal iron intake is also no less significant during heavy menstruation, as this can result in greater loss of this mineral.

Enriched with Bromelain and MCT Oil

Yum Yum Whey also contains the vitamins B6 and B12, which help reduce both fatigue and exhaustion. Vitamin B6 itself is also involved in the regulation of hormonal activity. Last but not least, we must also mention the important role of magnesium. It contributes to the proper functioning of muscles, the nervous system and mental well-being.

Key Nutrients for Vitality

Another great advantage of this protein powder is its excellent and quick absorption rate (about 10 grams of protein per hour). This makes it an ideal candidate for the preparation of a post-workout recovery drink. That said, you can also add it to smoothies, your favourite porridge, desserts and other meals, which it will enrich with a fair serving of protein. For example, it will easily turn a cheesecake into a balanced dessert to go with your coffee.

No matter if your goal is to gain muscle mass, lose weight or stay in shape, in all these cases, it is essential that your diet contains enough protein to ensure the functioning of all vital processes. All the above makes Yum Yum Whey an effective choice that will help you meet the recommended daily protein intake and, as a result, get you closer to your goals.
